Philosophy

🌴 Palm grows where the sun meets the sea.

We believe orchestration should be:
• Simple at the core, powerful at the edges
• Human-first — interactive, resumable, and beautiful
• Truth-seeking & High-agency — explicit, pluggable, durable
• Built for the long run — transactional, persistent, extensible

Palm is a philosophical son — balancing structure with flexibility,
precision with humanity, and automation with mindful participation.
